Our Services

Open MRI

Our state-of-the-art open MRI machine provides a comfortable experience for patients while delivering high-quality imaging results. It is ideal for claustrophobic patients or those who need more space.

Ultrasound

We offer advanced ultrasound imaging, which is a non-invasive and painless way to examine organs and tissues inside the body, helping diagnose various medical conditions.

Digital X-rays

Our digital X-ray technology allows for faster and more accurate imaging, providing detailed visuals to assist in diagnosing injuries or medical conditions with minimal radiation exposure.
